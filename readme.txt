= How do i show ads? =

After the activation of the plugin, instead of entering your Clickbank ID in  the settings page of plugin, you are requested to enter your **MyCBGenie account number** in it.  
This step will ensure your Clickbank ID is linked on all of the products links in the Ads Display Blocks. To display ads, simply drag *MCG: Clickbank Affiliate Ads* on to your desired widget from 
 where you can play with your Ad apperance settings.
